<h2><strong>Elegant Weapon Repositions</strong></h2>
<hr /></center>
<p>A methodical and optimal weapon reposition mod, for both BaS and vanilla weapons. Introduces a lot of Rising Storm 2: Vietnam, Ground Branch and Escape from Tarkov inspired weapon positions to give them a more surreal approach to point aiming, as well as alternate aiming sights. Gone are the days when your weapon blocks half of your screen when in a ready and lowered position.</p>
<blockquote>
<h3>Requirements</h3>
</blockquote>
<p>You need the following add-ons in order for things to function properly and as intended:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.moddb.com/mods/stalker-anomaly/addons/blindsides-weapon-reanimation-and-rebalance-loner">Blindside's Weapon Reanimation and Rebalance - "Loner"</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.moddb.com/mods/stalker-anomaly/addons/lowered-weapon-sprint-animation">Lowered Weapon Sprint Animation</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.moddb.com/mods/stalker-anomaly/addons/boomsticks-and-sharpsticks">Boomsticks and Sharpsticks</a> (Only necessary for Optional BaS repositions)</li>
<li><a href="https://www.moddb.com/mods/stalker-anomaly/addons/alternate-aim-sights-for-eft-reposition">EFT Weapon Reposition</a> &amp; <a href="https://www.moddb.com/mods/stalker-anomaly/addons/eft-weapon-reposition-updated-22-fix-for-bas-25102022">EFT Weapon Reposition updated - Compatibility BaS fix</a> (Only temporary for now, as I had adjusted the initial weapons with this enabled so the desired effect isn't as expected)</li>
</ul>

<blockquote>
<h3>Installation</h3>
</blockquote>
<p>Install via MO2, using the FOMOD and select the appropriate mods you have installed. Place anywhere in the load order and launch your game.</p>
